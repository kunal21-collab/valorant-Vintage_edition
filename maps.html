MAPS

Emberfall Citadel

A volcanic fortress split by flowing lava channels and collapsing bridges. Tight inner corridors contrast with wide molten courtyards, demanding aggressive duels, vertical rotations, and precise timing to control the fiery central stronghold.

Shadowspire District

A neon-lit cityscape filled with elevated rooftops, dark alleys, and shifting barriers. Silent flanks, high-ground duels, and stealth pathways allow unpredictable rotations, rewarding smart positioning and shadow-driven ambush strategies

Frostveil Expanse

An icy research facility buried in a frozen valley. Sliding surfaces, brittle ice walls, and long-range sightlines create dynamic engagements, forcing players to balance cautious movement with bold pushes through blizzard-covered lanes

Obsidian Ruins

Ancient black-stone temples connected by hidden tunnels and collapsing platforms. Mid-control is vital as rotating through underground passages enables surprise attacks and unpredictable strategies in this mysterious, echo-filled battleground

Ironbound Harbor

A massive industrial dockyard filled with cranes, shipping containers, and open water hazards. Tight choke points blend with wide sniping lanes, rewarding coordinated pushes and creative utility usage to secure the central cargo zone.

Celestia Rift

A floating arena suspended above the clouds, broken into multiple hovering platforms and teleport bridges. Constant movement, risky jumps, and shifting elevations create high-speed combat and chaotic map control opportunities.
